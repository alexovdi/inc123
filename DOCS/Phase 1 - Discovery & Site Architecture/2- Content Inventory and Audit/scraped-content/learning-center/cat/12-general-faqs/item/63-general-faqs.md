What is the Bank Account Management Service? How does it work?

If you choose to opt for the "Bank Account Management Service", this is how it works:  
  
Once we have incorporated your new Nevada Corporation or Nevada LLC, a U.S. person is appointed and authorized to open an account in the company name. An independent accountant is appointed to manage the account and issues wire transfers and checks based on your instructions. As the client you will be provided access to the account via an automated telephone service. Unfortunately online access cannot yet be granted. For a description of the service please click on the next item or view the [Bank Account Management Service](/services/bank_account_management) in our services pages.
