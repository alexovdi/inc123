The LLC Structure - neither a partnership nor a corporation

An LLC is neither a partnership nor a corporation. It is a distinct entity created by statute that offers businesses a superior alternative to the old general partnership and more and more a great alternative to the traditional C-Corporation.

One person can form and run a Nevada Limited Liability Company (See: [The LLC Disregarded Entity](/learning-center/cat/21/item/62#body)). One person can hold the offices of President, Treasurer and be the sole Manager and sole Member. Many states require at least two members. Thus, there is no need to involve other parties in a Nevada LLC, unless the owner desires. Managers of LLC's need not be Members. Managers or Members need not live or hold meetings in Nevada, or even be U.S. Citizens.

We form "Manager Managed" LLC’s and then provide a "nominee" Manager, the ultimate in privacy. Your name is eliminated from Public Record Entirely!

You operate the LLC as an officer (non public record) giving you total operational control. You maintain total ownership control and ultimate overall control through confidential Nevada LLC membership. As "Nominee Manager" of your LLC we do not intend to involve ourselves in your business. The service is intended solely to eliminate your name from all State records and in doing so provide you the privacy you deserve.

The Membership of a "Manager Managed" LLC is not listed in the Secretary of State records! Whether you are an attorney or the IRS, you cannot determine the owner of a Nevada LLC by contacting the Secretary of State.
