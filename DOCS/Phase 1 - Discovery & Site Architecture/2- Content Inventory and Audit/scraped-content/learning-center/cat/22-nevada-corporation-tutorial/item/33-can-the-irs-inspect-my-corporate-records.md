Can the IRS inspect my corporate records?

During an audit the IRS will inspect your corporate minute book and without written documents to support the actions of the corporation, it may disallow any tax benefits resulting from those actions. This can produce a large tax liability only because you failed to take the few moments necessary to keep up-to-date corporate minutes. Don't you lose your valuable tax benefits. Make good corporate record keeping an essential part of your tax schedule.
