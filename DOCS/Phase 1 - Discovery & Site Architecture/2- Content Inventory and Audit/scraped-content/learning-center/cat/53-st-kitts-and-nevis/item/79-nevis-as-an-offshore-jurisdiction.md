Nevis as an Offshore Jurisdiction

The Nevis Limited Liability Company Ordinance of 1995 is a modern and comprehensive statute which includes many of the provisions found in Nevada LLC's. Download the [Nevis LLC Company Ordinance](/learning-center/NevisLLC_statute.pdf).  
  
The most important Asset Protection features that are similar to Nevada are:  

-   The "Charging Order Protection".  
    
-   Creditor that obtains a "Nevis Charging Order" will be subjected to US Taxation
-   Similar Taxation Options to US Companies
-   The ability to have only one member
-   Nevis will not respond to a supeona issued by a court other than a Nevis Court
-   Nevis will not co-opporate with Authorities of any country unless it involves a crime
