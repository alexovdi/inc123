# Homepage

> URL: `/` | Layout: Full-width single-column | Unique page

## Purpose

Intent router and first impression. Routes visitors to the right pillar (Privacy, Asset Protection, Formation) or directly to packages. Establishes credibility (25 years, Reno office, all-inclusive pricing).

## Layout

- Full-width, single-column
- No sidebar
- Persistent header (mega menu) + footer
- Mobile: sticky bottom CTA bar

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Split layout (headline + CTAs left, visual right). H1: "Privacy, Asset Protection & Business Formation — All-Inclusive, Nothing Hidden". Two CTAs: "Protect Your Privacy" + "View Packages". Trust strip below (25 years, Reno office, money-back).
2. **Pillar Navigation Cards** — 3 intent-routing cards (Privacy blue, Asset Protection green, Formation amber). Each with icon, headline, 2-3 bullet points, "Explore" CTA.
3. **Package Quick Links** — 4 cards: Wyoming Gold $1,275, Nevada Gold $1,800, Wyoming Silver $900, Nevada Silver $1,350. Each shows tier name, price, 3 key features, "View Details" CTA.
4. **Differentiator Grid** — Dark background section. 6 value propositions in 2×3 grid: 25 Years Experience, Year-Round Nominees, All-Inclusive Pricing, Offshore Privacy, Real Reno Office, Crypto Accepted. Icon + title + 1-sentence description each.
5. **Social Proof / Testimonials** — Carousel of 2-3 client testimonials with star ratings. Fallback: "By the Numbers" stats strip if no testimonials available.
6. **Trust Signals Bar** — 6 icon+label pairs: Since 2000, Specialist Team, Money-Back Guarantee, Phone Support, Crypto Accepted, Secure Checkout.
7. **How It Works** — 4-step horizontal process: Choose Your Goal → Select Package → We File Everything → You're Protected. Numbered circles with connector lines.
8. **FAQ Section** — 4-6 most common questions in accordion format.
9. **Final CTA Section** — "Ready to Protect Your Privacy?" + dual CTAs: "View Packages & Pricing" + "Schedule a Consultation". Phone number fallback.
