How long does it take to open the Bank account?

If you purchase the [Gold Incorporation Package](/services/gold-package), upon request, we will facilitate the opening of a Corporate or LLC bank account. We can generally arrange to open an account, and arrange for the Bank signature cards to be sent to you within 5 days after the company has been "organized".  
  
If you prefer, we can simply prepare corporate/LLC resolutions authorizing the account opening and appointing signatories so you can contact your "Bank of Choice" directly.
