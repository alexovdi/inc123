User Personas

Five primary personas mapped to conversion journeys — from high-value privacy seekers to price-conscious formation buyers.

🔒
Journey A · Privacy Pillar
The Privacy Researcher
Small business owner with $500K+ in assets. Has heard about anonymous LLCs and wants to understand options before committing. Will read detailed content and is willing to pay a premium for genuine privacy protection.
Path 4–7 pages
Target Gold Package
AOV $1,275–$2,100
Search Intent
"anonymous LLC" "hide LLC ownership" "private LLC formation"
Psychographic
Research-oriented, quality-focused, understands the value of proper structuring. Not an impulse buyer — will compare options thoroughly before purchasing.
/privacy/ → WY Privacy Cluster → Nominee Services → WY vs NV Compare → Gold Package
↗ 30–40% upsell to Asset Protection
📍
Journey B · Privacy Variant
CA/FL Resident Seeking Privacy
California or Florida business owner whose home state requires public disclosure. Needs a WY/NV entity combined with foreign registration back home — a higher-complexity, higher-value package.
Path 2–3 pages
Target CA/FL Private Package
AOV $2,500–$3,200
Search Intent
"California anonymous LLC" "private LLC Florida" "hide business ownership CA"
Key Insight
Needs a dual-state structure explained clearly — form in WY, register foreign in CA/FL. The Privacy Pillar must include a "Which state are you in?" selector routing them directly to state-specific packages.
/privacy/ → CA/FL Private Cluster → CA/FL Package → Checkout
🛡️
Journey C · Asset Protection Pillar
The Lawsuit-Averse Business Owner
Real estate investor or business owner who has been sued — or knows someone who has. Understands defense costs run $30K–$100K minimum and wants the strongest LLC protection available. May not initially realize they also need privacy.
Path 4–6 pages
Target Silver → Gold Upsell
AOV $1,275–$2,100
Search Intent
"asset protection LLC" "charging order protection" "best state LLC protection"
Psychographic
Sophisticated understanding of LLC structures. Needs education on charging order protection and the critical difference between privacy and asset protection. Enters planning Silver, frequently upgrades to Gold.
/asset-protection/ → Charging Order → WY AP Cluster → Cross → Privacy → Gold Package
↗ Highest-leverage upsell: "Hiding ownership is your first line of defense"
⚡
Journey D · Formation Pillar
The Informed Buyer
Already decided on state and entity type. Comparing providers on price, inclusions, and trust. May have been referred by an accountant or attorney. Wants to get it done quickly — every extra page in this path costs conversions.
Path 1–2 pages
Target Silver or Bronze
AOV $900–$1,575
Search Intent
"Wyoming LLC formation" "Nevada incorporation" Attorney / CPA referral
Key Insight
Must reach packages in one click from homepage, state hubs, or header nav. Speed is the priority. The strategic opportunity is contextual cross-sells — "Add privacy for $375 more."
Homepage / State Hub → Package Page → Checkout
↗ "Add privacy for $375 more" — upgrade Silver → Gold
🔍
Journey E · Formation Pillar
The Formation Researcher
Knows they need a business entity but hasn't decided LLC vs Corp or Wyoming vs Nevada. Researching options and comparing. Price-conscious initially but can be upsold on value once educated on privacy benefits.
Path 3–5 pages
Target Silver → Gold Potential
AOV $900–$1,575+
Search Intent
"LLC vs corporation" "Wyoming vs Nevada LLC" "best state to form LLC"
Key Insight
This is where Incorporate123 competes head-to-head with budget providers (ZenBusiness $0, LegalZoom $0–$299). Every formation page must differentiate on what's included vs. competitors' base price + hidden add-ons. 40%+ upgrade potential from Silver to Gold.
/formation/ → LLC vs Corp → WY vs NV Compare → Package Page → Checkout
↗ "70% of clients add nominee services"
