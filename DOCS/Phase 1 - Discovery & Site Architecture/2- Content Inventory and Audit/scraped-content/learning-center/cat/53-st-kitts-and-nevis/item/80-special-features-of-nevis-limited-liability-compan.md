Special Features of Nevis Limited Liability Companies

-   Members of an LLC may be individuals or business entities of any nationality or domicile.
-    No annual member reports are filed in the Nevis public record.
-    The LLC's records may be located anywhere in the world.
-    LLC's may amend their articles and merge with other domestic or foreign LLC's.
-    Foreign LLC's may transfer their domicile to Nevis.
-    LLC's may have limited life.
-    LLC members may assign their interests to other parties.
-    Sole member LLC's are permitted.
-    Management of the LLC may be by managers or members.
-    Members are not liable for the obligations of the LLC.
