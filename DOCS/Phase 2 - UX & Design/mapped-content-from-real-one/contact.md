## What to Expect

When you contact us, here is what happens:

1. **Initial discussion** — We ask about your goals, not just what you want to file. Understanding your business, privacy needs, and asset protection priorities allows us to recommend the right structure.
2. **Recommendation** — Based on your situation, we suggest a specific package and jurisdiction, explaining the trade-offs and costs clearly.
3. **No pressure** — We are advisors, not salespeople. If our services are not the right fit, we will tell you. If you need time to consider, take it.
4. **Clear pricing** — Every fee is disclosed upfront. Our packages include all state filing fees for the first year. There are no hidden charges, no second-tier fees that appear later, and no surprise costs.
