# Privacy incorporation SEO: a wide-open competitive landscape

**The privacy/asset protection LLC niche has remarkably weak organic competition.** Across 10 high-intent search queries central to Incorporate123's new positioning, the five analyzed competitors collectively rank only 5 times — meaning 90% of these SERP positions are held by law firms, legal publishers, and generalists, not dedicated formation services. Anderson Advisors is the strongest content competitor with 651+ blog posts and top-3 rankings for "anonymous LLC," but even they have major gaps in offshore structures, profession-specific privacy, and interactive tools. The budget benchmark ZenBusiness explicitly disclaims offering anonymous LLC formation, confirming the niche is deliberately abandoned by high-volume players. For Incorporate123, this creates a rare opportunity: **no single competitor owns the privacy incorporation content space**, and a focused content strategy can capture dominant positions across dozens of commercially valuable keywords within 12–18 months.

**Privacy/asset protection coverage — the critical finding:** ZenBusiness has just **2–3 pages** touching anonymous LLCs. **SERP performance:** Ranks **~#9 for "anonymous LLC formation"** — a lower page-1 position with informational content that cannot convert. No rankings for the other 9 target queries.

A niche player creating "Wyoming LLC for Privacy" or "New Mexico Anonymous LLC Guide" faces **no direct competition from these high-authority pages** because ZenBusiness's templated content is generic by design.
