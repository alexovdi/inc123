# Nevada Bronze LLC — $699 All-Inclusive

Affordable Nevada formation with all state filing fees and registered agent included. The Bronze package covers the essentials for business owners who need a Nevada entity without privacy features or office services.

## Why Choose Nevada Bronze?

Not every business formation requires a full-service package. The Bronze package exists for business owners who need a properly formed Nevada entity at the lowest possible price, with the confidence that all mandatory state fees are included and nothing will be billed after the fact.

This package is designed for two primary scenarios. First, business owners who are physically located in Nevada and already have a business address, office space, and local presence. If you are operating from Reno, Las Vegas, or elsewhere in Nevada, you do not need our office or mail forwarding services — you just need the entity formed correctly with a registered agent in place. Second, business owners who plan to immediately file a foreign registration in their home state and need the Wyoming or Nevada entity purely as a legal structure. In this case, the Bronze package plus a foreign registration add-on ($149) provides the most cost-effective path.

Nevada's structural advantages — no state income tax, no franchise tax, strong liability protections — apply to Bronze entities just as they do to Gold and Silver. The difference is in the services surrounding the formation, not in the legal standing of the entity itself. A Bronze LLC has identical legal authority to a Gold LLC under Nevada law.

Every state filing fee is included in the quoted price. This covers the articles of organization or incorporation, the mandatory Initial List of Officers, and the Nevada State Business License. There are no setup fees, no hidden charges, and no contracts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is included in "all state filing fees"?**
The Bronze price includes every fee charged by the Nevada Secretary of State for formation: articles filing, Initial List of Officers ($150), and State Business License (LLC $200, Corp $500). These second-tier fees are frequently excluded by competitors and billed separately.

**What is NOT included in Bronze?**
Privacy services (nominee officers/directors), offshore record storage, corporate minutes maintenance, annual report filing, mail forwarding, and office services. Bronze is a formation-only package. For ongoing compliance management, consider Silver. For privacy, consider Gold.

**Is a Bronze LLC legally different from a Gold LLC?**
No. The legal standing of your entity is identical regardless of which package you choose. The package tiers determine which services surround the formation — not the authority or validity of the entity itself.

**Who should choose Bronze over Silver?**
Business owners who are physically located in Nevada and do not need mail forwarding, office services, or compliance management. Also, business owners who have their own attorney or accountant handling ongoing compliance and just need the initial formation done correctly.

**Can I upgrade to Silver or Gold later?**
Yes. You can add compliance services, nominee services, and other features to an existing entity at any time. Starting with a higher tier is more cost-effective than adding services piecemeal, but upgrading is always available.

**Do I still need to file annual reports?**
Yes. Nevada requires an annual list of officers/managers and business license renewal each year. With the Bronze package, you handle this yourself or add our annual compliance service at renewal time. Missing these filings can result in your entity being revoked.

**How fast is formation?**
Standard Nevada processing takes 3-5 business days. Rush filing ($199 add-on) provides 24-hour processing.
