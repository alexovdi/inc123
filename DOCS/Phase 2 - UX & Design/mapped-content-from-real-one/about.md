# About Incorporate123

## Our History

Incorporate123 began in 2000 as a traditional Nevada incorporation service, helping entrepreneurs and business owners form corporations and LLCs in one of America's most business-friendly states. Within our first few years, we recognized that our clients needed more than just filing services. They needed the same level of privacy and asset protection that had previously been available only through expensive offshore service providers or high-end law firms.

We started building those capabilities into our domestic packages. Nominee services, enhanced registered agent protections, and careful structuring became core offerings rather than expensive add-ons. By 2009, we had begun internationalizing our services to provide an additional layer of protection that no purely domestic provider could match.

Today, we serve clients across the United States and internationally, offering formation services in Wyoming, Nevada, California, Florida, and four international jurisdictions: Nevis, BVI, Panama, and Hong Kong.

## Our Philosophy

We believe in a straightforward principle: if you are operating within the law, then the law should protect you. That includes protection from overreaching government agencies, aggressive litigants, and anyone else who might seek your confidential business information without proper legal authority.

We believe you have a right to conduct your personal and business affairs privately behind the legal protections of a corporation or limited liability company. We believe you have a responsibility to protect your assets in order to secure your family's future. And we believe your entity should be able to engage legal counsel to interact with any inquiring party, rather than having that party show up at your home or business.

This philosophy guides every decision we make, from how we structure our own company to how we design our service packages.

## Why We Internationalized

After the introduction of the U.S.A. Patriot Act in 2001, the landscape for business privacy changed significantly. Under the broad umbrella of anti-money laundering compliance, government agencies and large corporations began requesting client information from registered agents and service providers with increasing frequency, often through informal administrative requests that carried no legal compulsion.

Most Nevada service providers complied with these requests without question. We chose a different path. Beginning in 2009, we internationalized our corporate structure and operations to ensure that client confidentiality could be maintained to the fullest extent permitted by law in every jurisdiction where we operate.

This website is owned and operated by Fidelity Solutions Limited, incorporated in Hong Kong. Business formation and ancillary services are provided by approved service providers in Hong Kong, Panama, St. Kitts and Nevis, Wyoming, and Nevada. This structure means that client records are held under the legal protections of multiple jurisdictions, making it significantly more difficult for any single authority to access your information through informal channels.

We comply fully with the law in every jurisdiction. We do not complicate your business in any way. We simply provide the privacy that you should expect and that you certainly still deserve.
