## Refund Policy

We offer a **30-day, 100% satisfaction guarantee**. If you are not satisfied with our services for any reason, contact us within 30 days of purchase and we will refund your payment immediately. No questions asked.

**What is included in the refund:** All fees paid to Incorporate123 for the service package.

**What is excluded:** Any fees already paid to the state on your behalf (filing fees, business license fees, etc.) are non-refundable, as these payments are made directly to government agencies and cannot be recovered.

### Exceptions

**Banking Services**
We cannot guarantee that a bank will approve your corporate account application, as this decision is made by the banking institution and is outside our control. No refund will be issued due to a bank's decision to decline an account application.

**Shelf Company Purchases**
The refund policy does not apply to shelf company purchases. Once shelf company documents have been released to you (typically electronically), the purchase price is fully earned and non-refundable. Please ensure a shelf company is the right choice for your needs before purchasing.
