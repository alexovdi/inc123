What is International Asset Protection?

The principal goal of International Asset Protection is to place your assets outside of the jurisdiction of the court system where you are resident. This can be accomplished by moving funds internationally using international bank accounts and International Business Companies or it can be as simple as transferring stock of an existing U.S. Corporation to your newly formed International Asset Protection Trust.  
  
It is common to hear that "offshore planning requires substantial liquidity", usually because the Attorneys that require this, also require $10,000 - $50,000 in fees in order to provide you with the necessary Trust and Strategy. It is our goal to provide a (relatively) simple international asset protection structure, inexpensively, that will provide "Bullet Proof" Asset Protection.  
  
The most important International Asset Protection features are:  

-   Placing your assets beyond the jurisdiction of U.S. Courts and out of harm’s way
-   Extreme Offshore Privacy Solutions that eliminate your name from any public record
-   International Asset Protection Trusts (IAPTs) that allow you to "control everything, own nothing"  
    
-   International and domestic business entity formations
