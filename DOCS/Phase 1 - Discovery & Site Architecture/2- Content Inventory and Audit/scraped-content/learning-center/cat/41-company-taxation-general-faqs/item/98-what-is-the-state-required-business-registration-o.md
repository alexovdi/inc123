What is the State Required "Business Registration" or "Business License"?

State Business License Filing - $200.00 (until 30th June 2013, after which, it reverts back to $100.00 per year).  

Per NRS 360.780, Once Incorporated, a Nevada Entity is required to pay for a State Business License. The filing Fee is currently $200. We include this with ALL [incorporation packages](http://www.nevada123.com/services/incorporation-package). Most other incorporating companies either charge an additional $200.00 for this (after the fact) or they some-how ”forget” to tell you about this requirement in order to keep their prices artificially low. The fee is paid to the Secretary of State, when the "Initial List" is due, then again annually.
