Our Philosophy

We strongly believe in the Law: If you are operating within the law, then we believe that the law should be used to protect you, even from intrusive Governments and Corporations.  

We believe in Liberty and the Right to conduct your personal and business affairs privately and confidentially behind the “veil” of a Corporation or Limited Liability Company. We believe you have a right, in fact, a duty to protect your assets in order to protect you and your family’s future. We believe that your Entity should be able to hire legal counsel to interact with any inquiring Government Agency, or other Party, rather than the Agency or Party showing up at your home or business to question or harass you!

We are passionate about this! We are unwavering and are not limited to thinking “inside the box”.  

Since 9/11/2001 and the introduction of the U.S.A. Patriot Act, and under the guise of anti-money laundering, Governments and Big Corporations have continually demonstrated an ever increasing disregard for your personal and business privacy. Most Nevada service providers have simply "rolled over" and "shared" their clients’ private information upon receiving a simple (and often times not even legal) "administrative request" from ANY Government Agency or powerful Corporation. Our conscience would not allow us to "roll over", so instead we "internationalized" our services in order to give you the privacy that you should expect and that you certainly still deserve.
