Executive Mail Forwarding Service  
[Kingsbury Executive Suites LLC](http://www.kingsburyexecutivesuites.com/basic_virtual.html) will provide you a prestigious [Lake Tahoe](http://www.kingsburyexecutivesuites.com/basic_virtual.html), Nevada address and 3 times a week mail is re-packaged in a confidential envelope and forwarded anywhere in the U.S., postage included. Since postage is included the service is limited to 75 pieces of mail per month. International clients using this service will be sent mail only once per week. The Company + 4 DBAs can receive mail as well as 2 officers/employees in their personal names.  

Package Forwarding  
All you need to do is provide [Kingsbury Executive Suites LLC](http://www.kingsburyexecutivesuites.com/executive_virtual.html) with a UPS or FedEx account number and packages received in your corporate name will be automatically forwarded directly to your "forward to" address. Save hundreds or thousands of dollars in Sales Tax on products you order online! We are not set up to handle large volumes of packages. We must therefore retain the right to discontinue this service for a client at any time. For our purposes a "package" is defined [here](/services/package-definition).  
  
Re-Mailing  
Re-Mailing is often overlooked, but may be one of the most important aspects of establishing a Nevada "Executive Suite". You forward out-bound mail to us "postage paid" and sorted per USPS regulations. We then deliver it to the local Post Office for mailing, where it receives a Nevada Postmark. This is an invaluable service for many of our clients. We are not set up to handle large volumes of re-mailing. We must therefore retain the right to discontinue this service for a client at any time.
