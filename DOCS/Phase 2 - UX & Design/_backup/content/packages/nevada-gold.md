## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why is Nevada Gold more expensive than Wyoming Gold?**
Nevada charges higher state filing fees than Wyoming. The Initial List of Officers ($150) and State Business License (LLC $200 / Corp $500) are mandatory Nevada requirements that do not exist in Wyoming. Our price includes all of these fees. Many competitors exclude them and bill you separately after formation.

**Should I choose Nevada or Wyoming for privacy?**
Wyoming generally offers stronger structural privacy at a lower cost because it requires fewer public filings. Nevada is the right choice if you have a specific reason to be incorporated in Nevada — such as physical operations in the state, existing business relationships, or contractual requirements. If you are choosing purely for privacy with no Nevada-specific need, Wyoming Gold is typically the better option.

**What are "second-tier" filing fees?**
These are Nevada-required filings beyond the basic articles of organization: the Initial List of Officers/Managers ($150) and the State Business License (LLC $200, Corp $500). Most discount formation services do not include these, resulting in unexpected costs after formation. Our Gold package includes all of them.

**Will my name appear on any Nevada public records?**
No. With year-round nominee services, our nominees appear on every filing — the articles, the Initial List, the business license application, and all annual renewals. Your name is never entered into the Nevada Secretary of State database.

**Do I need a Nevada physical address?**
Nevada entities need a registered agent with a physical Nevada address, which is included in this package. If you want a business mailing address for correspondence or to establish stronger Nevada presence, our Virtual Office add-on ($149/year) provides a professional Reno, NV address.

**How long does Nevada formation take?**
Standard Nevada processing takes 3-5 business days. Rush filing ($199) moves your filing to 24-hour processing. The complete package — including Initial List and Business License — is typically delivered within 5-7 business days on standard processing.

**Is the Nevada Business License the same as a local business license?**
No. The Nevada State Business License is a state-level requirement separate from any county or city license. Our packages use a registered agent address in a jurisdiction that does not require a county business license, avoiding an additional layer of public filings and fees.

**Can I start with Nevada Silver and upgrade to Gold later?**
Yes, but adding nominee services after formation requires amending existing filings with the Secretary of State, which incurs additional fees. Starting with Gold is more cost-effective if you know you need privacy.
