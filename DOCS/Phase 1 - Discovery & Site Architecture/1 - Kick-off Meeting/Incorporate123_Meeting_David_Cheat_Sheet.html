❓ Four Decisions Needed from David
1. Primary Domain: .co vs .com?
Rec: Migrate to .com. Brand trust outweighs 1–3 month SEO dip.
URLs already restructuring — domain change is essentially free. One disruption, not two. All 20+ secondary domains → redirect to primary.
2. Package Tiers: Gold / Silver / Bronze?
Rec: Keep 3-tier. Gold = hero (privacy + nominees). Silver = entry. Bronze = NV price-sensitive.
Conversion architecture depends on upgrade paths. Confirm: naming, included services per tier, offshore integration.
3. Compliance: Full pillar or distributed?
Rec: Full pillar. 5 pages: hub, registered agent, annual reports, domestication, foreign reg.
Retention play for returning clients. Easy to collapse later; hard to reorganize if distributed.
4. Domain Portfolio: 20+ domains — consolidate?
Rec: All redirect to primary. No standalone content strategies.
Multiple domains dilutes authority. One strong domain with 71 pages beats five weak ones. Check backlink profiles first.
