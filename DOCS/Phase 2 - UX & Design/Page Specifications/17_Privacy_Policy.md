# Privacy Policy

> URL: `/privacy-policy` | Layout: Full-width single-column | Unique page

## Purpose

Legal requirement. Discloses how Incorporate123 collects, uses, and protects visitor/client data. Especially important for a privacy-focused company — this page must be thorough and genuine, not boilerplate. Linked from footer and checkout terms checkbox.

## Layout

- Full-width, single-column
- Max-width: 800px (narrow for readability)
- No sidebar, no CTAs (pure legal content)

## Sections

1. **Hero** — H1: "Privacy Policy". Last updated date. Brief intro: "At Incorporate123, privacy isn't just what we sell — it's how we operate."
2. **Information We Collect** — What data: name, email, phone, billing address, company details, payment info, browsing data (cookies, analytics). How: forms, checkout, cookies, analytics tools.
3. **How We Use Your Information** — Entity formation filing, customer communication, payment processing, service improvement, legal compliance. What we DON'T do: sell data, share with third parties for marketing, use for advertising.
4. **Information Sharing** — Who we share with and why: state filing offices (required for formation), payment processors (Stripe/PayPal), registered agent services. No marketing sharing.
5. **Data Security** — SSL encryption, secure payment processing, access controls, data retention policy.
6. **Cookies & Tracking** — What cookies: essential (session), analytics (Google Analytics or privacy-friendly alternative), no advertising cookies. Cookie consent banner behavior.
7. **Your Rights** — Access, correction, deletion requests. How to contact us. California/CCPA rights if applicable.
8. **Third-Party Links** — Disclaimer about external sites linked from the site.
9. **Children's Privacy** — COPPA compliance (services not directed at children under 13).
10. **Changes to This Policy** — How updates are communicated. Effective date.
11. **Contact** — How to reach us about privacy concerns. Email + phone + address.
