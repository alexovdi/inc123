## 1. Page Purpose & Strategic Role

### 1.1 Primary Goal

Legal compliance. Standard terms governing the use of the website and the purchase of Incorporate123's services. Establishes the contractual relationship between Incorporate123 (Nevada Corporation Services Ltd.) and its clients.

### 1.2 Strategic Considerations

- The Terms must clearly define what Incorporate123 **is and is not**: a formation service, NOT a law firm. This disclaimer limits liability and sets appropriate expectations.
- The 30-day money-back guarantee referenced on package pages must be formally defined in the Terms (scope, exclusions, process).
- Nominee service terms — what nominees do and don't do, liability boundaries, and ongoing obligations — should be referenced or defined here (or in a separate service agreement).

## 5. Content Notes for David

1. **Legal review** — Terms of Service require legal counsel review before publication. This spec provides structure, not legal language.
2. **Refund policy** — The 30-day guarantee terms defined here must match exactly what's stated on package pages. Any difference creates legal exposure.
3. **Nominee service terms** — Does a separate nominee service agreement exist? If so, the Terms should reference it. If not, nominee terms need to be included here.
4. **Governing law** — Nevada is assumed (Nevada Corporation Services Ltd. is a Nevada entity). Confirm.
5. **Crypto refunds** — How are refunds handled for cryptocurrency payments? Refund in crypto at current value, or refund in USD equivalent?
6. **Auto-renewal** — Do annual renewals auto-renew? If so, the Terms must disclose this and provide a cancellation process. The brand promise of "no auto-renewing subscriptions you didn't authorize" (from the About page philosophy) must be consistent with the actual Terms.
