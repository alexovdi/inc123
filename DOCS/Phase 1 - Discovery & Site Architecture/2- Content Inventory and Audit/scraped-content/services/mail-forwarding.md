Nevada Basic Weekly Mail Forwarding

[Kingsbury Executive Suites LLC](http://www.kingsburyexecutivesuites.com/basic_virtual.html) will provide a prestigious [Lake Tahoe](http://www.kingsburyexecutivesuites.com/basic_virtual.html), Nevada address to receive your First Class Corporate Mail and Courier Delivered Letters. Once a week mail is re-packaged in a confidential envelope and forwarded anywhere in the U.S. or Canada, postage included. Since postage is included the service is limited to 30 pieces of mail per month and DOES NOT INCLUDE [PACKAGES](/services/package-definition). Mail is forwarded to international clients once monthly. This service meets the needs of most of our clients. The Company + 2 DBAs can receive mail. Officers may not receive mail in their personal names.  
  
This service is included in the [Basic Virtual Office](/services/nevada-office) package, if Executive Mail Forwarding (a higher level of service) is required, please consider the [Executive Virtual Office](/services/executive-virtual) package.
