## Frequently Asked Questions

**What does "year-round nominees" mean?**
Unlike services that only use nominees during the initial filing, our nominee officers and directors remain in place on every subsequent annual report and state filing. Your name never appears on the Wyoming Secretary of State's public database at any point during the life of your entity.

**Will I still control my LLC or Corporation?**
Yes. Nominees are listed on public records only. You retain full operational control through your operating agreement (LLC) or through private corporate resolutions (Corporation). The nominee arrangement is documented in private agreements that are not filed with the state.

**What is offshore record storage?**
Your corporate record book — including membership lists, stock ledgers, and meeting minutes — is maintained outside U.S. jurisdiction. This adds a layer of protection against domestic subpoenas seeking to identify the beneficial owners of the entity.

**Do I need to live in Wyoming?**
No. The majority of our clients live and operate in other states. If you plan to conduct business in your home state, you will need to file a foreign registration there, which we offer as a $149 add-on.

**Are all state filing fees really included?**
Yes. The $1,275 (LLC) or $1,575 (Corporation) price includes every fee charged by the Wyoming Secretary of State for formation. There are no surprise invoices after checkout.

**How long does formation take?**
Wyoming typically processes electronic filings within one business day. Most clients have a fully formed entity within 24-48 hours of placing their order. Rush filing is available for same-day processing.

**What is the difference between Gold and Silver?**
The Gold package includes year-round nominee officers and directors plus offshore record storage — these are the privacy features. The Silver package includes formation, registered agent, and compliance services but does not include any privacy protection. If keeping your name off public records matters to you, Gold is the right choice.

**What states can I foreign-register in?**
We handle foreign registration in all 50 states. If you operate in a state other than Wyoming, you will likely need to register there. Foreign registration is available as a $149 add-on.

**Is my annual renewal price locked in?**
Annual renewal pricing is subject to change, but we guarantee 30 days' notice before any price adjustment. State filing fees are pass-through costs set by the state.

**Can I upgrade from Silver to Gold later?**
Yes, but it is more cost-effective to start with Gold. Adding nominee services after formation requires additional state filings to change the officers and directors on record, which incurs extra fees.
