Architecture Overview
This document presents the finalized 71-page site architecture for the Incorporate123 website redesign. The architecture has been validated against: 392 crawled URLs mapped to destinations, 5 competitors analyzed for structural gaps, 3 user journeys confirmed through flow analysis, and content migration data identifying priorities. Every page, URL, and structural decision documented here has a traceable rationale.

Pillar 2: Asset Protection
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Secondary Differentiator  --  9 pages :: Hub URL: /asset-protection/
Asset Protection targets high-net-worth individuals ($500K+ in assets), real estate investors, and business owners in litigious industries. This pillar educates on the critical distinction between privacy (protects identity) and asset protection (protects wealth), creating natural cross-sell opportunities to the Privacy pillar.
