## Global Content Rules

These rules apply across every section of the homepage. They come from the Brand Guide v2 and Positioning Framework and are non-negotiable.

### Voice

| Attribute         | On the Homepage                                                                                                       |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Authoritative** | Cite specific numbers: $1,275, 25 years, $525/yr renewal. Never vague claims.                                         |
| **Educational**   | Explain outcomes (what privacy achieves) not mechanisms (how nominees work). Save mechanism details for pillar pages. |
| **Transparent**   | Show real prices. Show renewal rates alongside formation prices. No "starting at."                                    |
| **Reassuring**    | Emphasize track record, real office, ongoing support. Never create anxiety.                                           |
| **Precise**       | Every number is specific and verifiable. "25 years" not "decades." "$1,275" not "around $1,300."                      |

### Terminology

| Always Use                   | Never Use                            | Reason                                                             |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| "Business Incorporation"     | "Company Formation"                  | David's preference — more American-sounding                        |
| "All-inclusive"              | "Starting at"                        | Brand promise: no hidden fees                                      |
| "Year-round nominees"        | "Nominee services" (alone)           | Differentiates from "nominee for a day" competitors                |
| "Privacy"                    | "Anonymity" (as lead term)           | Privacy is the positive framing; anonymity can feel evasive        |
| "$1,275" (or specific price) | "Affordable" / "competitive pricing" | Premium positioning — never compete on price                       |
| "Protect Your Privacy"       | "Hide Your Identity"                 | Active/positive vs. passive/negative framing                       |
| "Business Privacy"           | "Anonymous LLC" (as lead term)       | "Anonymous LLC" is a cluster keyword, not the pillar-level concept |

## Section 1: Hero

**Wireframe ref:** Section 1 in Wireframe Spec
**Personas served:** All five (this is the universal entry point)
**Strategic job:** Establish the repositioned identity in <3 seconds. Route by intent OR shortcut to packages.

### Headline

| Parameter           | Specification                                                             |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Length**          | Max 8 words                                                               |
| **Tone**            | Authoritative, outcome-focused, privacy-first                             |
| **Must convey**     | This company protects your privacy. This is not a generic filing service. |
| **Must NOT convey** | Fear, urgency, geography-first, entity-type-first                         |

**Direction options** (David picks or writes his own — these are starting points):

- "Privacy and Protection for Your Business"
- "Your Business. Your Privacy. Our Expertise."
- "Business Privacy Starts Here"
- "Protect Your Identity. Protect Your Assets."

**Anti-patterns to avoid:**

- ✗ "Form Your Nevada LLC Today" — geography-first, entity-first
- ✗ "The Best Incorporation Service" — vague, unsubstantiated
- ✗ "Affordable Business Formation" — competes on price
- ✗ "Don't Risk Your Assets" — fear-based

### Subhead

| Parameter        | Specification                                                |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Length**       | Max 25 words (1–2 lines)                                     |
| **Must include** | At least one specific number (years, entity count, or price) |
| **Tone**         | Supportive, trust-building, specific                         |

**Direction options:**

- "25 years of specialized experience in Wyoming and Nevada business privacy, asset protection, and incorporation. All-inclusive pricing — no hidden fees."
- "Year-round nominee services, offshore records storage, and all-inclusive formation packages. Trusted since 2000."
- "[X] entities formed over 25 years. Wyoming and Nevada privacy and asset protection specialists."

**Prototype placeholder:**

## Content Production Workflow

This section clarifies how homepage copy moves from this guide to the live site.

| Step | Who         | What                                                                   |
| ---- | ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | BIT Studios | Delivers this Content Direction Guide with placeholder copy            |
| 2    | David       | Reviews direction, confirms or adjusts messaging preferences           |
| 3    | BIT Studios | Builds homepage prototype using placeholder copy                       |
| 4    | David       | Writes final copy for each section using this guide as the brief       |
| 5    | BIT Studios | Integrates final copy into prototype, adjusts layout if lengths change |
| 6    | David       | Reviews and approves final homepage with real copy                     |

**Key principle:** David writes or approves all final copy. BIT Studios provides strategic direction, placeholder content, and SEO specifications — not finished prose. The placeholder copy in this document is designed to be good enough for prototyping but is explicitly not the final version.
