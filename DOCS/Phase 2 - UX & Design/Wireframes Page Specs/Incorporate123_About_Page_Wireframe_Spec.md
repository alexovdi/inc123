## 1. Page Purpose & Strategic Role

The About page carries disproportionate trust-building weight for Incorporate123 — because the company launches with zero public reviews, this page must do the heavy lifting that a Trustpilot widget would normally handle. It needs to establish David's 25-year expertise, prove the Reno office is real, show the human behind the business, and communicate why Incorporate123 exists in a way that resonates with privacy-conscious buyers.

This page is also the single best opportunity to tell the company's story in a way that differentiates from both budget filing mills (faceless automation) and premium consultancies (corporate suits behind gated phone calls). The tone should be: expert, approachable, transparent, real.

### Primary Objectives

- Establish David as a credible privacy and formation expert with 25 years of specialized experience
- Prove the Reno office is real (not a P.O. box, not a virtual address)
- Communicate the company's origin story and why it focuses on privacy
- Build trust that compensates for zero third-party reviews at launch
- Differentiate from both budget mills and corporate consultancies
- Provide a human connection point that makes visitors comfortable spending $1,275+

### Target Audiences

| Audience | Why They're Here | What They Need |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Pre-purchase researcher | Visited package page, wants to verify legitimacy before paying | Proof of real office, real person, real experience |
| Comparison shopper | Evaluating Incorporate123 vs. competitors | Differentiation story, expertise signals, 25-year track record |
| Referred visitor | Accountant or attorney sent them here | Professional credibility, specialization evidence |
| General trust-seeker | Navigated here from footer or nav | Company story, physical address, phone number, founder info |

## 4. Founder Section

The single most important trust element on the page. David is the company — his expertise and accessibility are the brand.

### Layout

**Desktop:** Side-by-side — professional photo left, bio content right.
**Mobile:** Stacked — photo top (centered), bio below.

### Content

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Photo | Professional headshot of David (placeholder: avatar silhouette with "Photo pending" note) |
| Name | "David [Last Name]" (to be confirmed) |
| Title | "Founder & Principal" |
| Location | "Reno, Nevada" |
| Experience | "25+ years in privacy-focused business formation" |
| Bio Paragraph 1 | Origin story: how David started Incorporate123 around 2000, focusing on Wyoming and Nevada privacy formation at a time when most incorporators were geography-only services. What drew him to privacy and nominee services specifically. |
| Bio Paragraph 2 | Expertise: what David has built over 25 years — the nominee service infrastructure, the offshore records custodian capability, relationships with the Wyoming and Nevada Secretary of State offices. Why clients get a specialist, not a generalist. |
| Bio Paragraph 3 | Philosophy: David's approach to client service — real phone calls, direct access, no gatekeeping behind sales consultations. Why he accepts cryptocurrency. Why the pricing is transparent. |
| Direct Contact | "Call David directly: (775) 555-0123" with tap-to-call + email link |
| Trust Signals | "25+ years · Reno, NV office · Direct access — no call center" |

### Content Principles for Bio

- Written in third person ("David has..." not "I have...")
- Specific, not generic ("25 years forming Wyoming and Nevada entities" not "decades of experience")
- Human, not corporate ("answers his own phone" not "committed to client excellence")
- No fear-based language — confident, not alarmist
- Establishes credibility through specifics, not superlatives

## 11. Page Layout

Full-width single-column layout (no sidebar). The About page is a narrative — it reads top to bottom like a story.

**Desktop:** Single column, max-width 1000px, centered. Founder section uses 2-column layout for photo + bio.
**Mobile:** Single column, stacked.

### Section Order

1. Breadcrumb
2. About Hero
3. Founder Section (photo + bio)
4. Company Story / Timeline
5. What Makes Us Different (3 philosophy cards)
6. Office Section (address, photos, map)
7. By the Numbers (stats strip)
8. Testimonials (if available, otherwise omit)
9. CTA Section
10. Footer

---

## 12. Mobile-Specific Requirements

### Founder Photo on Mobile
Centered above bio text. Not full-width — scaled to ~200px wide for a professional headshot treatment.

### Timeline on Mobile
Vertical single-column timeline with dots/lines on the left, content blocks on the right. Each milestone is a compact card.

### Office Photos on Mobile
Single photo or horizontal scroll if multiple. Map placeholder full width.

### Stats on Mobile
2×2 grid, large numbers centered in each cell.

### Sticky Bottom CTA Bar
"View Packages" + phone icon. Same as other pages.
