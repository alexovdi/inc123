1. Overview

Purpose

Shortcut the education funnel (4+ page reads) into a 60-second guided recommendation. Visitors who already know their intent can get a package recommendation in 1–3 clicks.

Placement

New section between HomepageHero (dark) and PillarCards (cream). Uses cream-alt background. Creates a “two paths” moment: guided quiz vs. self-directed browsing.

UX Pattern

Card-selection with auto-advance. No radio buttons, no dropdowns, no “Next” button. Click a card → slide to next question. 3-dot progress indicator. Back navigation available.

5. Edge Cases & Shortcut Paths

Non-standard flows that deviate from the 3-step pattern.

⚠

Bronze + Wyoming Fallback

Wyoming does not offer a Bronze package. When a user selects Formation → Basic Filing → Wyoming, the wizard:

  1. Recommends Wyoming Silver ($875) as the primary result
  2. Displays note: “Wyoming’s most affordable option is the Silver package at $875.”
  3. Shows Wyoming Gold and Nevada Bronze as alternatives
⚡

Shelf Company Shortcut (1 step)

Clicking “I Need a Shelf Company” in Step 1 immediately shows the recommendation screen. No tier or state questions — shelf companies are a distinct product category.

Result: Shelf Companies (From $2,000) with Wyoming Gold and Nevada Gold as alternatives.

⚡

CA/FL Shortcut (2 steps)

Clicking “I Operate in CA or FL” goes to a state-pick screen (California or Florida), then immediately shows the recommendation. No tier question — CA/FL Private packages are single-tier (Gold-equivalent).

Result: California Private or Florida Private ($1,475) with the other state’s Private package and Wyoming Gold as alternatives.
