Nevada Business Incorporation

This area is intended for Business Incorporation Only. Although Limiting your Business Liability and protecting your Assets are closely linked, this page is primarily for those who intend to operate a Business. If you are simply looking to protect personal assets such as: Cash, investment securities, notes, receivables, investment real estate, a personal residence, patents, trademarks, copyrights and shares in a closely held corporation,then please read the [Asset Protection](/services/asset-protection) pages.

Why Incorporate your Business?

-   To create an Entity separate and apart from its Stockholders/Members
-   Stockholders/Members are not liable for debts of Corporation/LLC
-   Protect your Personal Assets from Business Liability
-   Protect yourself from Personal Bankruptcy due to Business Failure
-   Increased Credibility with “Corporate Image”
-   Build Corporate Credit
-   Corporation/LLC will continue after the death of Stockholders/Members
