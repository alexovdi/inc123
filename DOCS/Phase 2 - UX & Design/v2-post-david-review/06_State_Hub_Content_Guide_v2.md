## Wireframe Improvement Notes

### 1. Intent Cards Need Stronger Visual Hierarchy — Privacy First

The wireframe shows a 2×2 grid with equal card sizing. But the strategic priority is Privacy → AP → Formation → Compliance. The Privacy card should be slightly larger or positioned first in a featured layout to guide visitors toward the highest-value conversion path.

**Recommendation:** On desktop, consider a featured layout: Privacy card at full-width or 60% width on top, then AP + Formation side by side below, then Compliance as a compact bar. On mobile, keep the stacked order but give Privacy card a subtle "Most Popular" or "Start Here" badge.

### 2. State Facts Strip Is a Strong Pattern — Add a "Why [State]?" Anchor

The hero's state facts strip ("No State Income Tax · $62 Annual Report · Strongest Privacy Statutes · Filing Fee ~$100") is excellent but static. Adding a small "Learn more about Wyoming ↓" anchor link to the "Why Wyoming?" section below would connect the two and reduce scroll friction.

**Recommendation:** Add a subtle "Why Wyoming? ↓" text link after the facts strip that scrolls to Section 6.

### 3. CA/FL Hubs Need Very Different Content Than WY/NV

The wireframe is built around Wyoming, but California and Florida hubs serve a fundamentally different purpose. CA/FL visitors aren't forming entities IN those states — they're forming in WY/NV and registering to operate in CA/FL. The intent cards, package links, and content map all need to reflect this "form elsewhere, operate here" model.

**Recommendation:** For CA/FL hubs, restructure the intent routing: Card 1 should be "Privacy from CA/FL Disclosure Laws" (how to form in WY for privacy while operating in CA/FL), Card 2 should be "CA/FL Foreign Registration" (how to register your WY entity), Card 3 should be "CA/FL-Specific Packages" ($2,500–$3,200 all-inclusive). The Compliance card stays similar.

### 4. Missing Consultation CTA After Package Section

The Package Quick Links section shows Gold and Silver cards but lacks a "Not sure which? Talk to us" safety net.

**Recommendation:** Add "Need help choosing? Call (775) xxx-xxxx" below the package cards.
