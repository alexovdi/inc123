Definition of a "package"

For mail forwarding purposes a "package" is defined as follows:  
  
U.S Domestic destination:  
Anything weighing more than 2 pounds OR anything larger than 12 x 14 inches.  
  
Canadian destination:  
Anything weighing more than 1 pound OR anything larger than 12 x 14 inches.  
  
Worldwide destination:  
Anything weighing more than 6 ounces OR anything larger than 12 x 14 inches.
