# Compare Packages & Pricing

Every Incorporate123 package includes all state filing fees for the first year, registered agent service, and custom records preparation. No hidden fees, no surprise second-tier filings, no costs that appear after you commit. Below is a complete overview of every domestic and international formation option we offer.

## Wyoming Packages

Wyoming offers the lowest formation costs and strongest privacy protections of any U.S. state. No state income tax, no franchise tax, and the most protective charging order statutes in the country.

### Wyoming Gold — Full Privacy
Includes nominee officer/director/manager, registered agent, records preparation, company secretary, EIN, and bank account opening.
- **State Filing Fee:** $100
- **Privacy:** Full (nominee services included)
- **Virtual Office:** No (Wyoming does not require business license or office)
- **Best for:** Clients who want maximum privacy with lowest ongoing costs
- [View full Wyoming Gold details](/packages/wyoming-gold/)

### Wyoming Silver — No Privacy
Includes registered agent, records preparation, company secretary, EIN, and bank account opening. No nominee services.
- **State Filing Fee:** $100
- **Privacy:** Basic (your name appears in public filings)
- **Virtual Office:** No
- **Best for:** Wyoming formation without privacy requirements
- [View full Wyoming Silver details](/packages/wyoming-silver/)

## Nevada Packages

Nevada provides a dedicated business court, 50+ years of business-friendly case law, and virtual office options for a credible Nevada business presence. Higher filing fees than Wyoming but unmatched judicial track record.

### Nevada Gold (Basic Virtual Office) — Full Privacy
Includes nominee officer/director/manager, basic virtual office with weekly mail forwarding, registered agent, records preparation, company secretary, EIN, and bank account opening.
- **State Filing Fee:** LLC $425 / Corporation $725
- **Privacy:** Full (nominee services included)
- **Virtual Office:** Basic (address, weekly mail forwarding, no packages)
- **Best for:** Privacy-focused businesses needing a Nevada address
- [View full Nevada Gold details](/packages/nevada-gold/)

### Nevada Gold (Executive Virtual Office) — Full Privacy + Office
Everything in Gold Basic, upgraded to executive virtual office with mail forwarding three times weekly (including packages), exclusive suite number, and part-time office use.
- **State Filing Fee:** LLC $425 / Corporation $725
- **Privacy:** Full (nominee services included)
- **Virtual Office:** Executive (suite number, package forwarding, office use)
- **Best for:** Businesses needing a professional Nevada presence with full services
- [View full Nevada Gold details](/packages/nevada-gold/)

### Nevada Silver (Basic Virtual Office) — No Privacy
Includes basic virtual office, registered agent, records preparation, company secretary, and bank account opening. No nominee services.
- **State Filing Fee:** LLC $425 / Corporation $725
- **Privacy:** Basic (your name appears in public filings)
- **Virtual Office:** Basic
- **Best for:** Nevada formation with an address but without privacy needs
- [View full Nevada Silver details](/packages/nevada-silver/)

### Nevada Silver (Executive Virtual Office) — No Privacy + Office
Everything in Silver Basic, upgraded to executive virtual office with enhanced mail and package forwarding.
- **State Filing Fee:** LLC $425 / Corporation $725
- **Privacy:** Basic
- **Virtual Office:** Executive
- [View full Nevada Silver details](/packages/nevada-silver/)

### Nevada Bronze — Essential Formation
Minimum service level: formation, registered agent, records preparation, and company secretary. No privacy services, no virtual office, no address.
- **State Filing Fee:** LLC $425 / Corporation $725
- **Privacy:** None
- **Virtual Office:** None
- **Best for:** Local Nevada businesses that do not need privacy or a virtual office
- [View full Nevada Bronze details](/packages/nevada-bronze/)
