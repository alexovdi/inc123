Only One Person & No Residency Required

One person can form and run a Nevada Corporation or Nevada Limited Liability Company (See: [The LLC Disregarded Entity](/learning-center/item/62/#body)). One person can hold the offices of President, Secretary, Treasurer and be the sole Director or for a LLC be the Manager and sole Member. Many states require at least three officers and/or directors. Thus, there is no need to involve other parties in a Nevada corporation, unless the owner desires. Directors and Officers of Nevada Corporations need not be Stockholders and Managers of LLC's need not be Members. Stockholders, Directors, Officers, Managers or Members need not live or hold meetings in Nevada, or even be U.S. Citizens. If you require we offer a [Nominee Officer/Director Service](/services/nominee) for ultimate privacy: Your name will not appear on any public record.
