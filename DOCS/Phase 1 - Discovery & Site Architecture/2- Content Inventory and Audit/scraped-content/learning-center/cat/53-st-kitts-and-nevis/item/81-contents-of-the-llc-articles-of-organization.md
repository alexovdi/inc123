Contents of the LLC Articles of Organization

The Standard LLC Articles of Organization will state the following:  

-    The name of the LLC which must contain the words “Limited Liability Company” or the abbreviation “LLC” or “L.L.C.”
-   A statement that the LLC is formed under the Nevis Limited Liability Company Ordinance 1995.
-   The latest date on which the LLC is to dissolve (optional).
-   The statutory registered agent’s address in Nevis.
-    A statement whether the LLC is managed by managers exclusive of the members or by all of the members in their capacity as members.
-   Any other provision not inconsistent with Nevis law which the organizers elect to appear in the articles of organization.
