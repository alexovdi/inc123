# Incorporate123 — Domain Portfolio Discussion

## Overview

During the kickoff discussions, the topic of alternate domains and their potential SEO implications for the redesign project was raised.

**Resolution (March 2026):** Domain confirmed — `incorporate123.co` remains the primary domain. `incorporate123.com` redirects to `.co` via 301.

---

## Key Discussion Points

**Domain Strategy (Confirmed):**
`.co` is the primary domain with full SEO history. `.com` and all other owned domains redirect to `.co` via 301 redirects. No domain migration is needed — the redesign proceeds on the existing `.co` domain, preserving all accumulated authority and rankings.

**BIT Studios' Assessment:**
- Pointing multiple domains to a single site **no longer provides SEO benefit**. It only helps if people already know those domains or if valuable content is being created on them separately.
- The `.co` domain retains 25 years of SEO history and backlink equity. Staying on `.co` eliminates any migration risk entirely.
- With **301 redirects** from all secondary domains to `.co`, link equity from any domain variant is captured.
- Decision confirmed with Irish (BIT Studios' SEO lead).
