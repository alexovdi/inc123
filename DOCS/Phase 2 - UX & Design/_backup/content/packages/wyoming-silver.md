## Why Choose Wyoming Silver?

Wyoming offers real structural advantages for business formation regardless of whether you need privacy services. There is no state income tax, no franchise tax, and annual report fees are among the lowest in the country. The state's LLC and corporation statutes are modern, well-tested, and favorable to business owners. These benefits apply to every Wyoming entity, including Silver-tier formations.

The Silver package is built for business owners who want the legal and tax advantages of a Wyoming entity but do not need nominee officers, nominee directors, or offshore record storage. This is common for entrepreneurs who are comfortable having their name on public filings, who are forming an entity for a specific business venture where ownership is not sensitive, or who plan to operate primarily within Wyoming itself.

Many of our clients choose Silver when they are forming a Wyoming entity to serve as a holding company for real estate, to take advantage of Wyoming's favorable LLC statute for operating agreements, or to establish a business presence in a zero-income-tax state. If you later determine that privacy services would be valuable, upgrading to Gold-tier nominee coverage is always possible.

Every state filing fee is included in the quoted price. You receive a complete, formed entity with registered agent service, compliance filings, and professionally prepared organizational documents — with no hidden fees and no contracts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the difference between Silver and Gold?**
Gold includes year-round nominee officers and directors (keeping your name off public records) plus offshore record storage. Silver includes formation, registered agent, and compliance but your name will appear on Wyoming state filings as the owner, officer, or manager.

**Will my name be on public records with the Silver package?**
Yes. Without nominee services, your name will appear on the articles of organization (LLC) or articles of incorporation (Corporation) filed with the Wyoming Secretary of State. This information is publicly searchable.

**Can I upgrade to Gold later?**
Yes. We can add nominee services to an existing entity, though it requires filing amendments with the state to replace your name with nominee names on the public record. Starting with Gold is more cost-effective if you anticipate needing privacy.

**Do I need to live in Wyoming?**
No. Most of our Silver clients live in other states. If you plan to do business in your home state under this entity, you will need a foreign registration ($149 add-on).

**Are all state filing fees included?**
Yes. The quoted price includes every fee charged by the Wyoming Secretary of State. There are no additional charges at checkout or after.

**How fast is formation?**
Wyoming processes electronic filings within one business day in most cases. With rush filing ($199 add-on), you can have a formed entity the same day.

**What if I just need a basic filing with no extras?**
Silver is our entry-level Wyoming package. It includes registered agent, compliance, and document preparation. If you only need a bare-minimum state filing, please call us to discuss your specific needs.

**Is the annual renewal mandatory?**
Wyoming requires an annual report to keep your entity in good standing. If you do not file, the state will administratively dissolve your entity. Our renewal service ensures this never happens.
