Do I have to file a US company tax return/company audit?

For most companies the answer is yes at the Federal level. The filing is made with the Internal Revenue Service. If a company is a [Single Member LLC](/learning-center/item/62/#body) taxed as a [Disregarded Entity](/learning-center/item/62/#body) then the Member (not the company) will have the tax filing requirement. For types of LLC taxation please [click here.](/learning-center/item/37/#body)

Generally most client companies will NOT have a Nevada Tax Filing requirement. For exceptions please [click here.](/learning-center/item/21/#body)
