# About Page

> URL: `/about` | Layout: Full-width single-column | Unique page

## Purpose

Trust-building page. Establishes credibility through company story, expertise depth, physical office, and direct human contact. Critical for high-value prospects doing due diligence before purchasing Gold packages ($1,275+).

## Layout

- Full-width, single-column (no sidebar)
- Founder/team section uses 2-column: photo left, bio right (desktop); stacked (mobile)
- Max-width: 1000px

## Sections

1. **Breadcrumbs** — Home > About Us
2. **Hero** — H1: "25 Years of Privacy Expertise — Specialist Team, Proven Results". Subheadline emphasizing real people, real office. Office/team photo.
3. **Team Expertise Section** — 2-column layout. Professional photo left, bio right. Company voice (not individual founder bio per v2 change). Title, location (Reno, NV), experience line. 3 paragraphs: origin story, expertise/infrastructure, philosophy/service. Direct contact: "Call us directly: [phone]".
4. **Company Timeline** — Vertical timeline: ~2000 Founded → ~2003 Wyoming expansion → ~2005 Nominees launched → ~2008 Offshore structures → ~2012 Free offshore nominees → ~2015 Crypto accepted → ~2020 20th anniversary → 2026 Website redesign. Alternating left/right on desktop.
5. **"What Makes Us Different"** — 3 philosophy cards:
   - "Specialists, Not Generalists" — WY and NV only, 25 years depth beats 50-state breadth
   - "Transparent, Not Gated" — All pricing on website, no "free consultation" sales pitch
   - "Real People, Not Automation" — Real person answers phone, specialists know your name
6. **Office Section** — Address (Kingsbury Executive Suites, Reno), phone, hours, photos (exterior + interior). "Minutes from the Nevada Secretary of State — we hand-deliver filing documents." Map placeholder.
7. **"By the Numbers" Stats Strip** — 4 large numbers: "25+" Years | "2" States (WY + NV) | "$525" Annual Renewal | "100%" Transparent Pricing. Use entity count instead of "100%" if David provides it.
8. **Testimonials** — 2-3 real client quotes if available. Omit entire section if none — do NOT fabricate.
9. **Final CTA** — "Ready to Work with Us?" + "View Packages & Pricing" + "Contact Us" + phone.
