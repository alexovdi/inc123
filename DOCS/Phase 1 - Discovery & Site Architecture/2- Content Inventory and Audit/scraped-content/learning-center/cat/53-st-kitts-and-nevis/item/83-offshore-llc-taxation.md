Offshore LLC Taxation

Our services are not intended to assist U.S. persons in evading U.S. income tax. Our service includes obtaining an IRS Tax I.D. for the Nevis LLC.  
  
The Offshore LLC taxation "default classifications" are different than for domestic LLC's, but the fact remains that the offshore LLC can be taxed either as a C-Corporation in the U.S. or the income may "pass through" to a "single member" or to "multiple members" as "partnership" taxation. The members may be individuals, corporations or other LLC's.  
  
Because the Nevis LLC obtains an IRS Tax I.D. the usually necessary "controlled foreign corporation" tax return and other complicated filings required by U.S. owners are completely unnecessary. No need for any "tax evasion" concerns, but you may operate your business and/or protect your assets "with extreme prejudice!"
