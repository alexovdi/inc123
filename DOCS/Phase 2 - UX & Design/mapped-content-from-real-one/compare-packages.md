## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does formation take?**
Formation typically completes in 5-7 business days after we receive your order. Wyoming is the fastest at 1-2 business days. Nevada takes 3-5 business days for standard processing. Dual-state packages (California Private, Florida Private) take 7-10 business days total.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, Litecoin, and Monero). Contact us about payment arrangements for larger packages.

**Can I upgrade my package later?**
Yes. You can upgrade from Silver to Gold at any time. We will apply a prorated credit for your current package toward the upgrade.

**What if I need to dissolve my entity?**
We can handle voluntary dissolution filings in any state. Contact us for current pricing and timeline.

**Do I need a business license in addition to my LLC?**
The LLC formation is a state filing. Local business licenses, permits, and tax registrations are separate and depend on your business type and location. Nevada packages include the state-level business license, but county or city licenses may be required separately.

**Can I use my LLC to open a business bank account?**
Yes. We provide all the documents you need: articles of organization, EIN confirmation, and operating agreement. Most banks require these three items.

**What states can I foreign-register in?**
We handle foreign registration in all 50 states. If you operate in a state other than your formation state, you will likely need to register there. Foreign registration is available as a $149 add-on with any package.

**Is my annual renewal price locked in?**
Annual renewal pricing is subject to change, but we guarantee 30 days' notice before any price adjustment. State filing fees are pass-through costs set by the state.

**What happens if I miss my annual report deadline?**
Most states impose late fees and may administratively dissolve your entity. Our compliance monitoring sends reminders well before deadlines to prevent this. Packages that include annual compliance filing handle the deadline for you.

**Can I transfer an existing LLC to your management?**
Yes. We can take over registered agent, nominee, and compliance services for existing entities. Contact us for a transfer assessment.

**How do international packages differ from domestic?**
International entities (Nevis, BVI, Panama, Hong Kong) are formed outside U.S. jurisdiction and are designed for asset protection and international business. They require additional compliance (IRS reporting, KYC administration) and carry higher formation and renewal costs. Domestic packages are simpler to maintain and better suited for U.S.-based operations.
