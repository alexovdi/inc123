The Nevada Limited-Liability Company - praised for its organizational flexibility

The Nevada Limited-Liability Company, or LLC, has quickly become one of the most popular entities in the United States. Once seen as a daring corporate hybrid, the LLC is now praised for its organizational flexibility and innovation. If organized correctly, it offers liability protection and ownership privacy, as well as tremendous flexibility in the areas of taxation and management, including the ability to have only one member.
