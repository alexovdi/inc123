Refund Policy

With our 30 Day 100% Satisfaction Guarantee, ordering our services is completely risk-free. We are confident that you'll be delighted with our products and customer service, but if you are not satisfied for any reason, just let us know within 30 days of purchase, and we will refund payment immediately (exclusive of any fees already paid to the State). No questions asked!

Exception to refund policy regarding Banking Services: Please note however that we cannot guarantee that you will be able to open a corporate bank account as this process is out of our control and conditional upon the Bank's policies and procedures. No refund will be issued due to a banking issue.  

Exception to refund policy regarding a Shelf Company purchase:  
The Refund Policy (above) DOES NOT apply to the purchase price of a "[Shelf Company](/services/shelf-companies)": Once the Shelf Company documents have been released from us to you (usually electronically) the purchase price is deemed fully earned and NO REFUND will be issued.
